## Tips

1. **Title**: Keep under 60 characters for best display
2. **Description**: Keep under 160 characters
3. **Image path**: Always starts with `/img/` (relative to static folder)
4. **Keywords**: Optional, but helpful for SEO
5. **Test**: Always test with social media debuggers after adding
